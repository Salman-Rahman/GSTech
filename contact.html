About Us

Our customers places orders, in our website, we receive and review the order, then you forward it to your supplier. Our supplier then handles the packing and shipping, sending the product directly to our Warehouse.
Then through our logistics company we send the products to the respective country and deliver it to the customer. we manage the communication with the customer, ensuring they're informed about the order status and satisfied with their purchase. And there you have it! This is how our dropshipping works.
